Motswako Band Ignites Music Scene with Explosive New Release ‘10’

By Tsiunyane Sechaba | March 2026

Motswako Band has once again proven why they are at the forefront of the music scene with the release of their latest single, “10”. The track, featuring the dynamic talents of Jayson, Paynkilla, and Stymely, delivers an electrifying mix of rhythmic beats and lyrical dexterity that fans are already calling a masterpiece. The release has generated immediate buzz across social media platforms, highlighting the band’s ability to consistently innovate and captivate audiences.

Bars and Punclines

The collaboration brings together four distinct styles, creating a song that feels both fresh and cohesive. Jayson lends his smooth, melodic vocals, giving the track a soulful undertone. Paynkilla, known for his sharp and energetic flow, injects a punchy intensity that drives the song forward. Meanwhile, Stymely contributes a unique rhythm and lyrical flair, rounding out the track with a dynamic edge that keeps listeners hooked from start to finish

The music video accompanying “10” is equally impressive, featuring vibrant locations and creative visuals that complement the high-energy sound. Every scene is carefully choreographed, capturing the essence of the collaboration and the personalities of each artist. Fans have praised the video for its cinematography and storytelling, noting that it elevates the song to a full audio-visual experience.

'10' by Motswako Band: The Collaboration Everyone’s Talking About

Beyond its entertainment value, “10” also highlights the growing influence of the Motswako genre, blending traditional rhythms with modern urban sounds. The single serves as a testament to the versatility of the band and their featured artists, showcasing their ability to experiment while staying true to their musical roots. Critics are already hailing it as one of the most innovative releases of the year.

The release of “10” is also a strategic move for Motswako Band, demonstrating their ability to collaborate with rising stars and expand their audience. By joining forces with Jayson, Paynkilla, and Stymely, they are not only delivering a hit but also fostering a creative environment that nurtures emerging talent in the industry. It’s a bold statement that collaboration, when executed with skill, can elevate music to new heights.

Where to find the music

Fans can stream “10” on all major platforms, and the track is expected to dominate charts in the coming weeks. With its infectious beats, captivating performances, and stunning visuals, “10” cements Motswako Band’s place as a powerhouse in contemporary music. As the band continues to push boundaries, listeners can expect even more groundbreaking releases that challenge the norms of the Motswako genre.
